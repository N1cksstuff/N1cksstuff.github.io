Neon Gravestones

The citizens of Dema believe in Vialism, because it provides light to the City. There's a ceremony, where the nine Bishops trap the souls of the Glorious gone inside of Neon Gravestones. They get placed at the outskirts of Dema, providing light to the citizens, in a place where the sun doesn't shine. If you don't comply, you're not helping the City, not providing / a better future to your family, friends and the other Citizens. Sometimes I wonder if they're right. Maybe I am selfish, maybe the world would be better if my Body is rotting in a grave, whilst my soul shines Light for the people.

Sahlo Folina
